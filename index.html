Overview

Borneo is an island not a
country

The third largest island in the world, was once covered with dense
rainforests. With swampy coastal areas fringed with mangrove forests
and a mountainous interior, much of the terrain was virtually impassable
and unexplored. Headhunters ruled the remote parts of the island until a
century ago.
